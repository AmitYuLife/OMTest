## üé® Design Highlights

### Key Features

1. **Empathetic Hero**
   - Teal background (calmer than green for transition moment)
   - Clear message: "You're leaving your employer"
   - Supportive subtext: "Your benefits don't have to leave you"

2. **Value Visibility**
   - Shows what they've built: Pension (R75k), Life Cover (R850k), Disability (R425k)
   - Makes preservation tangible and real

3. **Three Clear Choices**
   - **Option 1 (Recommended)**: Stay with Old Mutual
     - Green highlight, gradient background, "Member-for-Life" badge
     - 4 benefits listed with check icons
   - **Option 2 (Warning)**: Cash out
     - Yellow border, shows consequences (tax, loss of cover)
     - Social proof: "Most people who cash out wish they hadn't"
   - **Option 3 (Neutral)**: Transfer elsewhere
     - Gray border, honest pros/cons
     - Shows it's okay, but not optimal

4. **Adviser Support**
   - Featured prominently at bottom
   - "Your adviser is here to guide you at no extra cost"
   - Channel-supportive, not bypass

5. **Clear CTA**
   - "Continue with Old Mutual" button
   - Green, prominent, easy to tap
   - Reinforces recommended path

## üß† Behavioral Design Principles

### Why This Design Works:

1. **Default Effect**
   - Option 1 visually pre-selected (green, filled radio)
   - Makes preservation the path of least resistance

2. **Loss Aversion**
   - Shows what you lose if you cash out (cover, future value)
   - More powerful than showing gains alone

3. **Social Proof**
   - "Most people who cash out wish they hadn't"
   - Peer behavior influences decisions

4. **Anchoring**
   - R75k today vs R450k+ at retirement
   - Future value anchors against present temptation

5. **Choice Architecture**
   - 3 options (not 2 or 10)
   - Recommended highlighted, not hidden
   - Alternatives transparent, not demonized

6. **Framing**
   - "Stay with Old Mutual" (continuity) vs "Cash out" (loss)
   - "Member-for-Life" (belonging) vs "Transfer" (starting over)
