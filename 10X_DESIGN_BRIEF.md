## 5. Modified Screen Specifications

All 8 screens from Old Mutual prototype can be adapted with these key modifications:

### Screen A: Unified Member Home
**Keep**: Core structure and layout  
**Modify**:
- 10X branding and color scheme (orange/coral accents)
- "Your 10X retirement savings" instead of pension terminology
- Emphasize fee transparency ("You're saving R[X] in fees vs industry average")
- Yu risk products shown as "Your Yu protection"
- Cross-sell tiles include: Retirement annuity, Unit trusts, Tax-free savings

### Screen B: Active Employment‚ÄîSmart Upsell & Cross-sell
**Keep**: Contextual prompts and life events approach  
**Modify**:
- Add 10X cross-sell products (RA, unit trusts, tax-free)
- Yu individual life insurance as upsell option
- Emphasize "low fee" benefit of adding products within ecosystem
- Show fee comparison vs traditional providers

### Screen C: Life Event / AI-Supported Insights
**Keep**: AI-powered scenario planning  
**Modify**:
- 10X investment strategy language (index-based, long-term)
- Show fee impact over time (10X's core message)
- Simple visualizations matching 10X's clean aesthetic
- "Based on 10X data and people like you..."

### Screen D: Employee Exit / Job Change Journey (CRITICAL)
**Keep**: Three-path preservation approach  
**Modify**:
- "Preserve with 10X" (highlighted option)
- Continue Yu risk cover as individual policy
- Show fee comparison: staying vs cashing out vs transferring
- Seamless transition to 10X preservation fund
- No adviser dependency (10X is more direct-to-consumer)

### Screen E: Continuation as Individual (Post-Employment)
**Keep**: Seamless transition messaging  
**Modify**:
- Transition to 10X retirement annuity as individual
- Yu individual life insurance product (new)
- Access to unit trusts and tax-free investments
- Same portal, new status
- Emphasis on "You're still saving on fees"

### Screen F: Pre-Retirement Guidance
**Keep**: Educational approach and scenario planning  
**Modify**:
- 10X investment philosophy (long-term, index-based)
- Fee savings compound visualization
- Link to 10X retirement calculators
- Preparation for living annuity transition

### Screen G: Post-Retirement Engagement
**Keep**: Sustainable drawdown focus  
**Modify**:
- 10X living annuity as the product
- Drawdown strategy aligned with 10X philosophy
- Fee transparency continues in retirement
- Yu protection continues (if needed)

### Screen H: Full Lifecycle Journey Map
**Keep**: Visual lifecycle approach  
**Modify**:
- 10X products at each life stage
- Yu risk products throughout
- Emphasis on continuous low fees
- Tech-enabled experience throughout
